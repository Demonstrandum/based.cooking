Coconut Oil Coffee

  • 🍽️ Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot coffee
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Blend coffee, coconut oil, and butter together in a blender until oil and butter are melted and coffee is frothy.
